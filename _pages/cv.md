Research Experience
======
* Jan 2025 – Present: Thesis Undergraduate Researcher in Computational Material Science
  * Haverford College, Department of Chemistry
  * Advisor: Dr. Clyde Daly
  * Performed Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations to model silica nanoparticle–peptide interactions and characterize adsorption at the nano-bio interface.
  * Implemented metadynamics sampling to calculate free energies of binding for unmodified and post-translationally modified peptides (glycosylated, phosphorylated).

* May 2025 – Aug 2025: NSF M-STAR Center Computational MXene Summer Undergraduate Researcher
  * Vanderbilt University, Department of Chemical Engineering
  * Advisor: Dr. De-en Jiang
  * Modeled novel tridentate amino ligands binding on hybrid MXenes (2D carbide material) using DFT calculations with geometry optimization and binding/reaction energy evaluation.
  * Performed *Ab initio* MD simulations to model deprotonation of organic (amine) and inorganic (MXene) frameworks.
  * Established and quantified tridentate ligand–MXene coordination, extending understanding from bidentate to polydentate binding motifs.

* May 2024 – Aug 2024: Computed Tomography (CT) Imaging Summer Undergraduate Researcher
  * University of Pennsylvania, Department of Bioengineering
  * Advisor: Dr. Peter Noël
  * Designed and 3D-printed heart phantoms mimicking coronary artery plaque environments.
  * CT-scanned ultra-high resolution and spectral images of phantoms from photon-counting CT.
  * Optimized reconstruction algorithms with Principal Component Analysis to enhance spatial resolution of spectral images.

* Jun 2023 – Jun 2024: Solid State Material Undergraduate Researcher
  * Haverford College, Department of Chemistry
  * Advisor: Dr. Alexander Norquist
  * Investigated copper-based perovskites using chloride/iodide precursors and under-documented amines from the Cambridge Crystallographic Data Center.
  * Conducted 70 bench-scale and 10 high-throughput reactions via Hamilton systems and visualized formation trends of novel copper halide perovskitoids.
